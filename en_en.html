Injetaq has its own headquarters with 8.000m2 of built-up area, located on land of 17.000m2, at Diadema/SP.

Injetaq can provide services individually, as well as a complete production process, which ranges from the development of a method plan, projects, making styrofoam models, making stamps and devices, with quality control of an international standard.

It provides services to the automobile, home appliance and toy industries, among others. With highly specialized equipment and manpower, the company is able to provide services in the areas of design and development, modeling, tooling, dimensional control and plastic injection, as well as making experimental prototype parts made of sheet metal or plastic.
